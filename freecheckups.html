Free checkups in hospitals are an important part of keeping the general public healthy and safe.

Free checkups can help catch illnesses early, which can help reduce the severity of the illness and the amount of money spent on medical care. Free checkups can also provide preventative care, which can help reduce the risk of more serious illnesses.

Hospital management can help facilitate free checkups by providing information about the services available, making sure the appropriate resources are available, and using appropriate staff to provide the checkups. This can help make sure that everyone has access to the healthcare they need.
